# Failed Scene Repair Boundary

## Scope

This boundary applies to the fallback stage:

```text
草稿诊断 -> 定位失败场景 -> 分场景再导演 -> 正文局部替换
```

The upstream chain `细纲 -> 执行包 -> 正文草稿` owns consistency, scene control, character action logic, information release, world rules, and hard causal structure.

The repair stage owns only the smallest failed scene or beat. Prose texture, de-AI feel, style fit, dialogue pressure, humor, rhythm, subtext, timing, and aftertaste should normally have been handled inside the first-pass generation chain.

## One-Sentence Rule

局部替换阶段允许对同一组场景事实进行分场景再导演，包括叙述顺序、详略、对白密度、时间伸缩、幽默落点、语言节奏和读者体验路径的调整；但不得新增、删除或改写执行包规定的场景事实、人物决定、因果关系和世界观约束。

## Scene Fact

A scene fact is a required event, object, action, decision, clue, document, payment, encounter, phone call, location, relationship state, knowledge state, or ending condition established by the outline, execution pack, or confirmed system material.

Do not add, delete, or alter scene facts during local repair.

## Narrative Presentation

Narrative presentation is how the same facts are experienced by the reader.

You may locally replace:

- Order of telling.
- Degree of detail.
- Time compression or expansion.
- Dialogue density.
- Action/psychology/description balance.
- Sentence rhythm.
- Comic timing.
- Emotional residue.
- Paragraph and beat structure.

## Rollback Boundary

If the prose draft is bad because required facts, motives, or scene logic are missing, do not invent the fix.

Flag the issue as:

- `需回退到细纲`
- `需回退到执行包`
- `需回退到正文草稿`

Then explain the reason briefly.
